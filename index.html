My Approach

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Our thoughts directly shape how we feel and act. In CBT, we look at your current habits and learn to actively spot and challenge the unhelpful or distorted thinking patterns that hold you back. It's about building a practical, day-to-day toolkit to reframe your perspective and change your response to situations.

Schema Therapy

When standard tools aren't enough, we need to look at the core beliefs you hold about yourself and the world. This approach targets long-standing emotional patterns and deeply ingrained coping mechanisms formed in early life. We pinpoint these root schemas so you can understand your triggers and build healthier ways of relating to yourself and others.

Person-Centered Approach

You are the expert on your own life. I provide a deeply empathetic, non-judgmental, and secure space where you don't have to wear a mask. Together, we work side-by-side to figure out your next steps at your own pace.
