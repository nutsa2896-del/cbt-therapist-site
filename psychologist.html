Psychologist Magda

As a practitioner, I look at our therapeutic timeline as genuine, collaborative teamwork. I don't operate as a detached observer analyzing you from afar. Instead, we pull up two chairs to map out patterns, test behavioral strategies, and target core cycles together.

My clinical lens incorporates Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Schema Therapy. This combination allows us to handle present-moment symptoms (like loops of panic or overthinking) while identifying older, deeper mental blueprints ("schemas") that make you prone to falling back into unhelpful traps like perfectionism or chronic self-doubt.

⚠️ Important: This website and its contact channels are not monitored 24/7 and are not equipped to handle emergencies. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact your local emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately.
