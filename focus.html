Who I Work With

My clinical practice serves individuals navigating distinct mental roadblocks, overthinking, or behavioral patterns. We match targets to actionable tracking tools:

Anxiety & Panic Loops

Deconstructing heavy physiological adrenaline loops, catastrophic predictions, and spatial avoidances.

Overthinking & Decision Paralysis

Breaking analytical patterns where your brain builds endless negative scenarios before simple actions.

Expat Burnout & Transition Strain

Handling identity disconnects, system adjustment exhaustion, and isolating feelings when relocating abroad.

⚠️ Important: This website and its contact channels are not monitored 24/7 and are not equipped to handle emergencies. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact your local emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately.
