Challenges We Can Work On Together

Therapy is a collaborative process designed to help you understand your emotional patterns and develop practical tools for daily life. While every individual's story is unique, these are some of the common challenges I help my clients navigate:

Anxiety & Stress Management

Quieting chronic overthinking, breaking out of loop-thinking, and learning how to ground your nervous system when feeling overwhelmed.

Depression & Low Mood

Navigating periods of feeling stuck, low motivation, emotional exhaustion, or a general sense of emptiness.

Sleep & Insomnia

Retraining your brain to break the cycle of bedtime anxiety, calming a racing mind at night, and rebuilding a natural, restorative sleep routine.

Self-Esteem & Self-Criticism

Softening a harsh inner critic, and building a more stable, compassionate relationship with yourself.

Expat & Life Transitions

Handling the hidden isolation, identity shifts, and unique career pressures that come with moving abroad or changing life paths.

Relationship & Communication

Identifying repetitive cycles in how you relate to others, setting healthy boundaries, and processing deep-seated emotional blocks.

Perfectionism & Burnout

Softening unrelenting standards, overcoming chronic work or academic exhaustion, and learning to disconnect guilt-free.
