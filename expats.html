For Expats

I'm an expat. Do you understand the unique challenges of living abroad?

Yes. Relocating resets your support network and can spark unique stressors, burnout, and adjustment friction. My practice focuses heavily on navigating these specific expat transition dynamics, identity disconnects, and cultural adjustment exhaustion.

Common Expat Challenges I Address:

  • Isolation and loss of familiar support networks
  • Cultural adaptation stress and language barriers
  • Identity shifts and sense of belonging
  • Relationship strain due to relocation
  • Career transitions and professional uncertainty
  • Reverse culture shock when visiting home
  • Anxiety about long-term relocation decisions

⚠️ Important: This website and its contact channels are not monitored 24/7 and are not equipped to handle emergencies. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact your local emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately.
